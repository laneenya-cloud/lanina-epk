// 01 — About

Bio

La Niña is a Taiwanese-American DJ based in New York whose sound was born on dance floors across New York City, Berlin, Barcelona, Madrid, and Taipei. That global influence is reflected in sets that are both high-impact and emotionally tuned, blending tech-house, techno, melodic house, and melodic techno into a sound built for movement and connection.

Following a breakout run through the NYC circuit, with standout sets at Elsewhere, 00:00, Delirium, and Ardent Noir Haus, La Niña has quickly established herself as a DJ with raw energy behind the decks and a signature sound shaped by the global language of electronic music.

Community sits at the heart of how she approaches music. The relationships she has built with fellow DJs, producers, and dancers across New York and beyond are what drive her growth as an artist and keep her connected to why she started playing in the first place.
